About Me

I’m a fan of sports, but mostly to watch. My favorite sport is soccer and I love watching all the games, even the ones of small teams that nobody knows about :) I also love playing video games! I play every time I can, in my phone, in my computer, on TV... I am very interested in aircrafts and dinosaurs. I enjoy listening to Podcasts about them and also about curiosities, economy, geography and history.

I graduated in IT almost ten years ago and I have done some endorsements since then, but I am very excited with the opportunity to refresh my skills and learn more in this course. One of the big challenges and goals that I have is to develop my speaking skills in English, although I understand it very well, I know that I have to work in my articulation abilities. I am looking forward to learn more about web development and work with this. I am a very patient and determined person and I believe these aspects will help me accomplish my goals.
